More about me

I'm a Msc. Astronomy student seeking to pursue a career in research. I did my undergraduate in Physics at the University of Barcelona with one semester abroad at Lund University. Currently I'm on a Studentship at the Isaac Newton Group of Telescopes on La Palma.

My research interests concern transiting exoplanets, atmospheric modelling and detection techniques. I enjoy modelling and data analysis with Python. I am also interested in observational astronomy and instrumentation.
